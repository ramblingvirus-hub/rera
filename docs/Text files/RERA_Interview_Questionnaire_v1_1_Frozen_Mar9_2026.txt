RERA Interview Questionnaire ñ Updated (Frozen Structure)
Date Frozen: March 9, 2026

Purpose: Structured buyer interview used by the RERA engine to assess potential risk signals in real estate transactions.

Project Information
Q1. Property reference or name (text).
Q2. Who is selling or developing the property? (text).
Q3. In which City or Municipality is the property located? (dropdown).
Q4. In which Province is the property located? (dropdown).
Q5. What type of property is being offered or sold? (Subdivision / Condominium / House and Lot / Vacant Land / Agricultural Land / Mixed?Use / Not Sure).
Q6. How is this property being offered for sale? (Developer Project / Private Sale / Broker Listing / Not Sure).
Developer Legitimacy (25%)
Q7. Has the developer shown a valid License to Sell (LTS)? (Yes / No / No documents shown / Not Sure).
Q8. Is the developer known to have completed other real estate projects before this one? (Yes / No / I cannot find information about the developer / Not Sure).
Project Compliance (30%)
Q9. Has the developer shown any local government approval or permit for developing this property? (Yes / No / No documents shown / Not Sure).
Q10. Has the developer shown an Environmental Compliance Certificate (ECC) or environmental approval for this development? (Yes / No / No documents shown / Developer says ECC is not required / Not Sure).
Title & Land (25%)
Q11. What type of land title or ownership document has been shown for this property? (TCT / CCT / Mother Title / Tax Declaration / Agrarian Reform Title / Rights Only / No title shown / Not Sure).
Q12. Are you aware of any claims, mortgages, disputes, or other issues affecting the property title? (No known issues / Issues disclosed / Seller claims title is clean but no proof shown / Not Sure).
Financial Exposure (10%)
Q13. Does the seller or developer offer in?house financing or installment payment plans directly through them? (Yes / No / Not Sure).
Q14. Are buyers asked to make reservation fees, deposits, or installment payments before key permits or ownership documents are shown? (Yes / No / Not Sure).
LGU & Environmental Context (10%)
Q15. Are you aware of any local government restrictions that may affect development in this area? (Yes / No / Not Sure).
Q16. Are you aware of environmental risks affecting this property (flooding, landslides, protected zones)? (Yes / No / Not Sure).
Future Enhancements (Not in MVP)
ï Document upload support (titles, permits, ECC, License to Sell).
ï AI?assisted document review.
ï Save?and?return interview workflow.
ï Buyer verification reminders for 'Not Sure' responses.


RERA ñ Phase 4A Planning: Interview Scoring & Risk Interpretation Layer
Date: March 9, 2026
Status: Design Locked (Implementation Pending)

Objective
Define the deterministic scoring architecture that converts the structured interview questionnaire
into a risk-based evaluation report. This phase introduces the interview scoring matrix, category
score calculation, risk band classification, and explanation engine.

Interview Questionnaire Status
The interview questionnaire structure has been frozen (RERA_Interview_Questionnaire_v1_1_Frozen_Mar9_2026).

Question Groups:
Q1ñQ6   Project Information
Q7ñQ8   Developer Legitimacy
Q9ñQ10  Project Compliance
Q11ñQ12 Title & Land
Q13ñQ14 Financial Exposure
Q15ñQ16 LGU & Environmental Context

Only Q7ñQ16 participate in scoring. Q1ñQ6 provide contextual metadata.

Risk Category Model
Categories and weights:

Developer Legitimacy ñ 25%
Project Compliance ñ 30%
Title & Land ñ 25%
Financial Exposure ñ 10%
LGU & Environmental Context ñ 10%

Engine keys:
developer_legitimacy
project_compliance
title_land
financial_exposure
lgu_environment

Category Scoring Logic

developer_legitimacy = (Q7 * 0.7) + (Q8 * 0.3)
project_compliance = (Q9 * 0.6) + (Q10 * 0.4)
title_land = (Q11 * 0.6) + (Q12 * 0.4)
financial_exposure = (Q13 * 0.4) + (Q14 * 0.6)
lgu_environment = (Q15 * 0.5) + (Q16 * 0.5)

All category scores range from 0ñ100.

Final Score Calculation

final_score =
(developer_legitimacy * 0.25) +
(project_compliance * 0.30) +
(title_land * 0.25) +
(financial_exposure * 0.10) +
(lgu_environment * 0.10)

Final score range: 0ñ100.

Risk Band Classification

80ñ100  Lower Risk
60ñ79   Moderate Risk
40ñ59   Elevated Risk
0ñ39    High Risk

Explanation Engine

The explanation engine generates three output groups:
1. Key Risk Signals
2. Information Gaps
3. Due Diligence Suggestions

Signals may be triggered by:
- Missing License to Sell
- No development permit shown
- Weak or unverified title
- Early deposit requirements
- Environmental hazards
- LGU restrictions

Report Output Structure

1. Final Score and Risk Band
2. Category Breakdown
3. Key Risk Signals
4. Suggested Due Diligence Steps
