**RERA – Privacy Policy (Philippine Data Privacy Act Compliant)**

Last Updated: March 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

RERA (Real Estate Risk Assessment Platform) complies with the **Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)** and its implementing rules and regulations.

This policy explains how personal data is collected, used, stored, and protected.

---

## 2. Personal Data Collected

### a. Personal Information

* Email address
* Account credentials

### b. User-Submitted Data

* Real estate project details
* Developer and property information
* Questionnaire responses

### c. System Data

* Evaluation results and risk scores
* Request identifiers (request_id)
* Usage logs

### d. Financial Data

* Credit transactions
* Payment references
* Subscription status

---

## 3. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed for:

* Generating risk evaluation reports
* Managing user accounts
* Processing payments and credits
* Maintaining audit and billing integrity
* Preventing fraud and abuse
* Improving system performance

Processing is limited to what is necessary for these purposes.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

RERA processes personal data based on:

* **Consent** – when users submit data and use the platform
* **Contractual necessity** – to deliver evaluation services
* **Legitimate interest** – for system security, fraud prevention, and audit integrity

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Personal data may be shared with:

* Payment processors (e.g., PayMongo)
* Infrastructure and hosting providers
* Government authorities when legally required

RERA does NOT sell personal data.

---

## 6. Data Retention

RERA retains data for as long as necessary for:

* Service operation
* Legal compliance
* Financial record keeping
* Dispute resolution

Important:

Certain data **cannot be deleted immediately**, including:

* Billing records
* Audit logs
* Evaluation records linked to financial transactions

These are required for system integrity and defensibility.

---

## 7. User Rights (Under RA 10173)

You have the right to:

* Be informed about data processing
* Access your personal data
* Correct inaccurate data
* Object to processing (where applicable)
* Request deletion or blocking
* File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)

Requests may be subject to system and legal limitations.

---

## 8. Security Measures

RERA implements:

* JWT-based authentication
* Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
* Immutable audit logging
* Access control enforcement
* Rate limiting and abuse detection

These safeguards protect against unauthorized access and data tampering.
